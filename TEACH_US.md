Company Memory > Better Models

Everyone is trying to get access to the newest models.
But I think the bigger advantage is keeping the knowledge your team already creates.
If someone solves a problem today, that learning shouldn't disappear in a chat or get lost after a few weeks. It should be easy for everyone in the company to find and reuse.
A team with average tools but great knowledge sharing will often move faster than a team with the latest tools and no shared learning.
Models will keep getting better.

What really compounds is what the team learns along the way.
